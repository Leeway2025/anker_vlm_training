四、各阶段数据文件格式与示例(逐字段)

4.1 euno 原始标注 json —— 客户输入(LlamaFactory 对话格式,1a 消费)

[
  {"id": "8000",
   "videos": ["trainset_4k_250828/5911b3bd-ae4d-4d1a-9aaf-91a1462a5ae6_segment_1"],
   "conversations": [
     {"from": "human", "value": "<video>You are an AI surveillance analyst. ...(生产 prompt 全文,所有样本相同)"},
     {"from": "gpt",   "value": "C|n|During the day, a man in light-colored clothes took a package from a residential doorway and left."}
   ]}
]
要点: videos[0] = index.json 的 key(定位 wds 分片);gpt.value = GT 串,分隔符为 无空格的 |(已 5/5 字节核对)。

4.2 DATA/labels.jsonl —— 通用主标签(1a 产出;3a/3b/4b/5/6/7b 全消费)

{"video_id": "trainset_4k_250828/5911b3bd-ae4d-4d1a-9aaf-91a1462a5ae6_segment_1",
 "video_uri": "trainset_4k_250828/5911b3bd-ae4d-4d1a-9aaf-91a1462a5ae6_segment_1",
 "duration_sec": null,
 "resolution": "384x384",
 "labels": {"role_type": "C", "sub_keyscene": "n",
            "description": "During the day, a man in light-colored clothes took a package from a residential doorway and left."},
 "meta": {"camera_id": "unknown", "storage": "wds",
          "wds_dir": "/data/anker_video_clips_wds_full", "shard": 15, "euno_id": "8000"}}
要点: ① meta.storage="wds" → 训练/推理自动走分片帧读取,无需 mp4;② camera_id 从文件名设备序列号(T8xxx)提取,uuid 命名 → "unknown" → 跑 camera_fingerprint 兜底(切分时 unknown 按单条处理);③ duration/原分辨率被上游预处理抹掉,固定 384x384;④ 代理集(视频文件)变体: meta 无 storage 字段,video_uri 为文件名,去 data.video_root 下解码。

4.3 gt_samples.txt —— 字节核对输入(1b;从数据文件原样复制,禁手敲)

D|g|A man in a hat approached a camera at a residence, paused, then turned and left.
B|i|Daytime at a residence, a male worker puts down a package, pauses, then picks it up and continues delivering.
E|p|At night, outside the residence, a bear knocked over a blue bucket placed on the porch.

4.4 pass1.jsonl —— Gemini 增强标注产物(2A;真实实测记录)

{"video_id": "trainset_ucsd/decrypt_T8030TEST0001_ped2_train001_segment_1",
 "gemini_model": "gemini-2.5-flash", "temperature": 0.1,
 "gemini_output": {
   "attributes": {"clothing": "casual", "held_items": "none", "posture": "walking",
                  "action": "walking", "view_type": "non_doorbell",
                  "sub_role_type": "Passerby", "dwell": "passes_through"},
   "reasoning_chain": "[Identity cues] Multiple pedestrians in casual clothes, no identifying attire [Scene cues] Outdoor walkway, normal passing traffic [Conclusion] Unspecified persons in routine activity → D|m",
   "predictions": {"role_type": "D", "sub_keyscene": "m",
                   "description": "Multiple pedestrians walk across a paved outdoor area from an overhead camera view."}}}
要点: ⓪ reasoning_chain 必须英文(2026-07-10 起校验器强制: 该文本是 5d 阶段 E2B 的 think 上下文,小模型英文强于中文且更省 token,中文超限自动重试);① attributes 七键的取值 = data/taxonomy.AUX_VOCABS 枚举(训练两端单一来源);② predictions 只用于 2A' 一致性过滤,不作训练标签(GT 全量信任);③ 失败记录写同名 .errors 文件(含 errors 字段),重跑自动重试。

4.5 filtered/whitelist_ids.txt(2A')与 4.6 资产文件(2A'')

# whitelist_ids.txt —— 每行一个 video_id;语义 = "该样本的 Gemini 增强标注可信"(非 GT 对错证据)
trainset_4k_250825/T8030P2322510409_T8160P2122501B2C_20230719_segment_1

# asset_A_attributes.jsonl —— Phase 5b 辅助头监督(仅白名单内条目)
{"video_id": "trainset_4k_250825/T8030P2322510409_..._segment_1", "confidence": 1.0,
 "attributes": {"clothing": "uniform", "held_items": "package", "posture": "walking",
                "action": "placing_item", "view_type": "doorbell",
                "sub_role_type": "DeliveryPerson", "dwell": "brief_stop"}}

# asset_C_reasoning.jsonl —— Phase 5d 隐式 CoT 目标(仅白名单内条目)
{"video_id": "trainset_4k_250825/T8030P2322510409_..._segment_1",
 "reasoning_chain": "[Identity cues] Uniform, walks straight to the door [Scene cues] Places a package on the doormat [Conclusion] Staff delivering a package → B|i"}
要点: 资产层已按白名单过滤 —— 训练样本永远全量;无资产/低置信(<0.5)样本自动降级: aux 标签 -100(该头 loss=0)、CoT 走生产模式,样本不丢。

4.7 训练目标串 —— 模型实际学习的 label 段(3b/4b/5 用 ①;6 用 ①+②混合)

① 生产模式(全阶段):
B|i|A uniformed delivery person places a package at the front door and walks away.
   ^^^^ RT字母+两个|+SK字母 → loss 权重 ×4;描述段 ×1;前缀 prompt 段全部 -100

② 推理链模式(仅 5d,占 60%,prompt 尾加 [REASON] 标记):
<think>[Identity cues] Uniform, walks straight to the door [Scene cues] places a package [Conclusion] B|i</think>
B|i|A uniformed courier places a parcel at the door and leaves.
   <think> 段 loss 权重 0(只作条件不反传);最后 0.5 epoch 强制全 ①(退火防泄漏)

4.8 preds_v0 / preds_v15.jsonl(4a / 4a' 推理产物)与 sw.json(4b)

# preds_*.jsonl —— 贪心解码原始输出;断点续跑按 video_id 去重追加
{"video_id": "trainset_.../..._segment_1", "output": "C|s|A person lingers near the front door at night."}

# sw.json —— hard mining 样本权重(video_id → 权重)
{"trainset_.../seg_1": 1.0, "trainset_.../seg_7": 3.0, "trainset_.../seg_9": 1.3}
要点: 1.0=答对样本;3.0=可信错例;分数权重(如 1.3)= per-class 膨胀封顶(1.5×)压缩后的值,物理复制用流式最大余数法保证总质量守恒。

4.9 kto_data.jsonl(7a → 7b)

{"video_id": "trainset_.../seg_1", "completion": "B|i|A courier leaves a parcel at the front door.", "label": true}
{"video_id": "trainset_.../seg_7", "completion": "D|m|A person walks past the porch casually.",      "label": false}
要点: label=true → desirable = GT 完整输出串(含 GT 描述,隐式保住描述质量);false → undesirable = v1.5 分类判错的原始输出(RT 或 SubKS 任一字母错即入选,纯规则判定零标注成本)。训练时每 batch 由分层采样保证混入错例。

4.10 euno 推理结果 json —— 基线复现输入(eval/euno_results_adapter 消费)

[{"id": "3", "video": "testset_1k_250730/00e22ff1-..._segment_1.mp4",
  "conversations": [{"from": "human", "value": "...(prompt)"},
                    {"from": "gpt", "value": "B|i|Daytime at a residence, a male worker puts down a package, ..."}],
  "pred": {"result": "B|i|A person in a blue vest approaches a walkway camera, ...",
           "score": [0.9472, 1.0]}}]
要点: adapter 拆成 preds.jsonl + gt.jsonl 喂 eval/metrics,即客户基线 93.60/76.14/86.22 的同口径复现路径。
